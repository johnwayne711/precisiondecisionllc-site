Users are solely responsible for evaluating all scan results and making their own trading decisions.

How to Delete Your Account

When the app account system is active, users will be able to initiate account deletion from inside the app under Account or Settings. Until then, or if a user cannot access the app, users may request deletion by contacting admin@precisiondecisionllc.com.

What Deletion Means

Account deletion may remove or anonymize account profile data, app preferences, and related account records where technically and legally possible.

What May Be Retained

Some information may be retained for legal, tax, accounting, fraud prevention, security, dispute resolution, or legitimate business purposes.

Subscription Cancellation

Deleting an account does not automatically cancel an active App Store subscription. Users must cancel subscriptions through Apple or the applicable app marketplace.
