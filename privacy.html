Privacy Policy for Options Terminal

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Options Terminal is operated by Precision Decision LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how Options Terminal collects, uses, stores, and handles information when you use the Options Terminal application, scanner workspaces, account features, watchlists, event workflows, support tools, and related Options Terminal pages.

Options Terminal is an informational options research and analytics tool. It does not provide brokerage services, custody, clearing, trade execution, money-transfer services, or personalized investment advice.

Information We Collect

Account and Authentication Information

If you create an account or sign in, Options Terminal may store account and authentication records such as:

  • your local account identifier;
  • your email address, display name, email verification status, and private-relay status when supplied by an authentication provider;
  • your sign-in provider and provider account identifier;
  • sign-in timestamps and account role or status information;
  • pending authentication request records needed to complete sign-in flows.

Options Terminal supports account sign-in flows through configured authentication providers such as Apple, Google, and email sign-in providers.

Usage, Entitlement, and Scanner Activity

Options Terminal may store records needed to operate usage limits, account access, and scanner activity, including:

  • entitlement state and source;
  • usage dates, analysis counts, and remaining-analysis calculations;
  • request identifiers;
  • ticker symbols or symbol lists submitted to scanners;
  • selected scanner or engine keys;
  • whether a usage request was accepted and the reason for the decision.

Watchlists, Alerts, and Preferences

If you use watchlists, Options Terminal may store watchlist names, watchlist order, saved symbols, watchlist alerts, alert read status, selected watchlist state, and related timestamps.

The app may also store UI preferences and workspace state in browser local storage, browser session storage, or Dash session stores. Examples include recently entered symbols, selected workspace mode, selected scanner, chart settings, layout state, theme preference, and recent symbol or detail selections.

Research, Market, News, and Event Data

When you use scanner, chart, event, or symbol-detail features, Options Terminal may process or cache market and event-related data such as:

  • option chains, expiration dates, underlying prices, implied volatility fields, and contract metrics;
  • earnings calendar information and earnings-window resolution results;
  • public non-earnings event records, source metadata, event dates or estimated windows, confidence scores, source URLs, and affected symbols;
  • stock-news article metadata when a configured news provider is used.

The reviewed app code uses Yahoo Finance/yfinance paths for market, price, expiration, and option-chain data; Nasdaq earnings calendar paths and local stored earnings data for earnings-calendar workflows; Finnhub company-news paths when configured for stock-news features; and official or public event-source adapters for supported non-earnings events.

Supported official event data sources include SEC EDGAR, FDA advisory committee pages, ClinicalTrials.gov, Federal Reserve FOMC calendars, BLS calendars, and BEA release-date data. These sources do not endorse Options Terminal, Precision Decision LLC, or any output produced by the app.

Support and Recommendation Submissions

If you submit feedback, a recommendation, bug report, or support message through the app, Options Terminal may store the submission type, message, optional ticker symbol, optional contact email, submission source/page, timestamp, and status.

Diagnostics and Logs

Options Terminal may create server logs, authentication diagnostics, error information, and diagnostic reports needed to operate, secure, debug, and improve the service. Authentication diagnostics are designed to avoid logging secret values.

The reviewed Options Terminal app code does not show advertising analytics. This statement is limited to the reviewed app code and does not make a claim about analytics or tracking that may exist elsewhere on the broader Precision Decision LLC website.

Payment Information

As of this update, the reviewed app code displays paid tiers and entitlement states, but in-app purchase, restore, cancel, and lifetime-purchase controls are placeholders and do not process payments or grant paid entitlement by themselves. Options Terminal does not collect payment card numbers in the reviewed app code.

If paid subscriptions or purchases are enabled later through an app store or payment processor, that provider may collect and process payment information under its own terms and privacy policy.

How We Use Information

Options Terminal uses information to:

  • create, authenticate, and manage user accounts;
  • operate scanner workspaces, Events workflows, Earnings workflows, watchlists, alerts, charts, and support tools;
  • enforce usage limits and entitlement states;
  • show saved watchlists, recent symbols, selected settings, and app preferences;
  • detect, prevent, and troubleshoot errors, misuse, unauthorized access, and security issues;
  • respond to support, feedback, and recommendation submissions;
  • maintain public event records, source attribution, and source revision history;
  • comply with applicable legal obligations and protect the rights, safety, and security of users and Precision Decision LLC.

When Information Is Shared

Options Terminal may share or transmit information only as needed to operate the service, including:

  • with configured authentication providers to complete sign-in and account-management flows;
  • with configured market, news, or event-data providers when a feature requires a data request, such as a symbol, option-chain, price-history, news, earnings, or public event-source request;
  • with an app store or payment processor if paid subscriptions or purchases are enabled;
  • with service providers that host, store, secure, or help operate the application;
  • when required by law, legal process, security needs, or to protect rights and safety.

Options Terminal does not provide brokerage, custody, clearing, trade execution, or money-transfer services.

Data Retention

Options Terminal retains information as needed to provide the service, maintain account and usage records, operate saved features, preserve source attribution and event records, resolve issues, support security and diagnostics, and comply with applicable obligations.

Retention may vary by data type, feature, source, backup, security need, dispute, and legal or operational requirement. This policy does not promise a fixed retention period.

Account Deletion

Signed-in users can request account deletion from the Account page in the app. The current account-deletion flow removes local account records, sign-in provider links, usage and entitlement records, watchlists, watchlist alerts, and saved recommendation submissions associated with the account. If configured and applicable, Options Terminal also attempts to remove the upstream authentication user and revoke Apple sign-in access associated with the account.

Deletion may not remove records that are not tied to the account, public or official event-source records, provider-controlled records, server logs, backups, legal records, or records retained for security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, compliance, or similar obligations.

Deleting an Options Terminal account does not automatically cancel any external subscription or purchase that may later be sold through an app store or payment processor.

Age Requirement

You must be at least 18 years old or the age of majority in your location, whichever is higher, to use Options Terminal.

Security

Options Terminal uses reasonable technical and organizational measures intended to protect the service and user information. No system can be guaranteed to be perfectly secure, and no transmission or storage method is risk-free.

Changes to This Policy

Precision Decision LLC may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted with a new Last updated date.
